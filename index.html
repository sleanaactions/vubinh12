Bảo tháp Lục Độ Đài Sen

Six Perfections Lotus Stupa
The architectural highlight of complex is Six Perfections Lotus Stupa, symmetrically arranged with Bodhi tree. Built at end of 20th century.
At the very top is the nine-tiered lotus pedestal (Cửu phẩm liên hoa), crafted from precious stone, symbolizing the nine supreme levels of the Pure Land.
Tower parameters
The stupa is fifteen meters high, consisting of eleven stories, with a floor area of about 10.5 square meters.
The stupa has a hexagonal shape. Each level features six arched doors, and within each doorway stands a statue of Amitabha Buddha carved from white precious stone.
